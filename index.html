About Us

Cioo forex academy is an online provider of digital educational products and services that teach essential Forex trading skills online and physically. The company leverages live interactive content supported by a comprehensive library of pre-recorded , live training sessions , seminars and E-books data and information to teach its students and struggling traders..

Cioo forex academy was founded in 2018, on November 18th as a small start up by an independent entrepreneur and forex expert with over four years experience, Oti Chukwudinma Onwuka, his vision is to provide an easily accessible online and physical platform for Forex education using a subscription model.
